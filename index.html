About Us

Verdaia Consulting Ltd. is a Zambian-based, globally connected consulting firm founded in 2024
with a strategic presence in Zambia & the Netherlands.

Specializing in Research, Development, & Consultancy, Verdaia Consulting Ltd.
delivers innovative & sustainable solutions across Life & Health Sciences, Engineering, Climate Change & the Environment.
